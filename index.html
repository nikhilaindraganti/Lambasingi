About Lambasingi

Lambasingi, also known as the "Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh," is a beautiful hill station situated at an altitude of over 1000 meters above sea level.

It is known for its cool climate throughout the year, and sometimes during winter, temperatures drop low enough to produce frost, giving it the nickname "Lambasingi Snowfall."

Activities

  • Trekking through scenic trails
  • Exploring coffee plantations
  • Visiting nearby waterfalls
  • Experiencing local culture and cuisine
